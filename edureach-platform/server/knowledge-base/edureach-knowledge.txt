EduReach College — Complete Information

ABOUT US
EduReach College is a premier engineering and technology institution established in 2005. Located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
The campus spans 25 acres with smart classrooms, advanced laboratories, central library with 50,000+ books, sports complex, and hostels.
Faculty includes 150+ experienced professors, many with PhDs from IITs. Student-to-faculty ratio is 15:1.
Ranked among top 50 engineering colleges in Telangana. Active alumni network of 10,000+ graduates worldwide.

B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) has 180 seats.
B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) has 120 seats.

FEE STRUCTURE 2024-2025
B.Tech Tuition Fee is Rs 1,50,000 per year. Hostel Fee is Rs 80,000 per year including mess. Lab Fee is Rs 15,000 per year. Exam Fee is Rs 5,000 per year. Total for Day Scholar is Rs 1,70,000 per year. Total for Hosteller is Rs 2,50,000 per year.
M.Tech Tuition Fee is Rs 1,00,000 per year. Hostel Fee is Rs 80,000 per year.
MBA Tuition Fee is Rs 2,00,000 per year. Hostel Fee is Rs 80,000 per year.

Scholarships: Merit Scholarship gives top 10 rankers 50 percent tuition waiver. Need-based Scholarship gives up to 100 percent fee waiver. Sports Scholarship gives 25 percent fee waiver for state or national level athletes. SC ST OBC students eligible for government fee reimbursement.
Payment can be made in 2 installments per year. Education loans available through SBI, HDFC, ICICI. EMI options available.

ADMISSIONS PROCESS
B.Tech: 70 percent seats through TS EAMCET or AP EAMCET counseling. 30 percent management quota based on 12th marks and interview. Eligibility is 10+2 with PCM minimum 60 percent. JEE Main holders get preference.
M.Tech: 50 percent through GATE score. 50 percent through college entrance test. Eligibility is B.Tech or BE with minimum 60 percent.
MBA: 70 percent through TS ICET counseling. 30 percent management quota. Eligibility is Bachelors degree with minimum 50 percent.
Application opens March 1st. Last date for management quota July 15th. Classes begin August 1st odd semester, January 2nd even semester.
